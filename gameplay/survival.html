Oxygen

The player need oxygen (O2) in order to survive. When diving, the player consumes an oxygen supply that depletes over time

Brain Coral

In Subnautica, brain corals produce bubbles that float to the surface and provide the player oxygen if they touch them. The bubbles of Brain Coral make splashes on the surface that appear to be flickering lights that follow an exceedingly consistent three-dot pattern at night.

PDA

Personal Digital Assistant

The PDA (Personal Digital Assistant), often known as the Survival PDA, is a multifunctional gadget carried by the player at all times. The PDA's AI monitors the player's health and will notify them whether they have 30 or 10 O2 points left, or if they are on the verge of dehydration or famine.

1

Inventory Paper Doll

The player may equip goods using a paper doll interface on the PDA's inventory tab.

2

Blueprints

The blueprints tab displays the player's current blueprints and their associated recipes.

3

Log Messages

While exploring Subnautica, you may come across abandoned PDAs. Collecting abandoned PDAs will cause their log messages to be downloaded to the player's PDA.

4

Databank

Every databank record obtained by the player is stored to the PDA's Databank tab. The AI will occasionally notify the player when a new databank entry is available and will occasionally describe the information.
