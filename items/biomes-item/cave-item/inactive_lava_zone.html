Inactive Lava Zone

Large, arching arches and walls of solidified lava with occasional boiling lava falls characterize the Inactive Lava Zone. It consists of two distinct sections and can be found in depths of 900 to 1300 meters, much below the limit of a Seamoth (even when equipped with a Seamoth Depth Module MK3), requiring an updated Cyclops or Prawn Suit to traverse through it. When navigating this biome, the player must be exceedingly cautious, as touching the lava on the Inactive Lava Zone's floor can quickly deplete the player's health, and the temperature causes continual damage unless the player has a Reinforced Dive Suit. It also has the uncommon mineral Kyanite, which is used in jewelry.

Depth Range: 900-1300 meters
