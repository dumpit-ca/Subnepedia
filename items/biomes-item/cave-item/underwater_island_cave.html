Underwater Islands Caves

Upside-down Coral Shell Plates, Spotted Dockleafs, Rubies, and Cave Crawlers tumble from the top to the bottom. The Underwater Islands Wreck is directly adjacent to the entrance of the smallest of these caverns. The biggest of the caverns is located on a nearby larger island. More materials and Cave Crawlers can be found in these larger caves. This cave has four openings and the smaller cave has three.

Depth Range: 205-255 meters
