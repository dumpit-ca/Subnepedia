Grassy Plateaus Caves

Drooping Stingers, for example, are known for their somewhat poisonous and bioluminescent vegetation.Rockgrubs and Shuttlebugs, as well as other cave fauna, can be spotted here. These caverns are also home to biters. These caverns include eight of the fourteen Sea Crowns on the map. There are currently three caverns where they can be found.

Depth Range: 120-245 meters
