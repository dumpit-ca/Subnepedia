Jellyshroom Cave

Below the Grassy Plateaus, Kelp Forest, and Safe Shallows is the Jellyshroom Cave system.At these locations, four entrances can be found: (130 -95 -390), (-360 -110 -225), (-495 -90 15), and (-725 -105 0).The area can be explored using the Seamoth, although due to the biome's depth, it is unsafe to do so without a Seamoth Depth Module MK1 or above. Due to the Crabsnakes' inclination to ambush the player from the Jellyshrooms itself, the Seamoth Perimeter Defense System upgrade is also recommended, though not required.

Depth Range: 180-300 meters
