Deep Grand Reef

The Deep Grand Reef is a huge cave system beneath the Grand Reef that is home to numerous hydrothermal vents. It is only accessible through cracks or holes in the Grand Reef and is far more perilous than it. The Cyclops could get around it, albeit the rock formations made it difficult.

Depth Range: 425-630 meters
