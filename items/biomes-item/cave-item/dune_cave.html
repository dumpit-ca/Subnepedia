Dunes Caves

Rockgrubs and Cave Crawlers, both Dunes residents, can be found in the Dunes Caves. These caves are only found in three locations. The presence of large resource deposits, such as Ruby and Lithium, is common here.In these caves, four of the map's fourteen Sea Crowns can be found.

Depth Range: 400-420 meters
