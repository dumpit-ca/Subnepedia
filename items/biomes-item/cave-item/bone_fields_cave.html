Bone Fields Caves

The Bones Field Caves are primarily gloomy, with little plant and coral to provide illumination, and little animals. Gel sacks are occasionally found in these caves, as well as an exclusive species of coral known as "Cave Coral."

Depth Range: 610-730 meters
