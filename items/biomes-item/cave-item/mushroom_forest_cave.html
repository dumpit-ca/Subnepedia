Mushroom Forest Caves

The Mushroom Forest Caves are a small cave system with typical Mushroom Forest life forms and coral formations, and two of them are home to Cuddlefish eggs.Grue Clusters, for example, are bioluminescent plants that guide the player through the caverns' various corridors. One alien arch can be located in this location, leading to the Primary confinement facility. The fungus sample can also be found in this section.

Depth Range: 170-250 meters
