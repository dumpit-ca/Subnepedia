Sea Treader's Tunnel Caves

The Sea Treader's Tunnel Caves are a vast labyrinth inside the tunnel that the Sea Treader Leviathans go through on Sea Treader's Path. The caves' entrances can be located both within (on the tunnel's walls) and outside (on the area's top). With the addition of Grue Clusters and an abundance of Ruby, it resembles the Deep Sparse Reef in terms of beautiful flora in general.

Depth Range: 200-340 meters
