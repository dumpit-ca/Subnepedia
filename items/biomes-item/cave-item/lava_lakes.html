Lava Lakes

In Subnautica, the Lava Lakes are a cave biome. They are the map's deepest biome, at around 1300 meters. It has the warmest ambient temperature of any biome, reaching up to 75 degrees Celsius. The Primary Containment Facility is located within the Lava Lakes. The tale of Subnautica revolves around this unique biome.The Inactive Lava Zone is the sole way to get to the Lava Lakes, which means that only the Prawn Suit with a Prawn Suit Depth Module MK2 and a Cyclops with a Cyclops Depth Module MK3 may go there. Lava Lakes and Lava Falls are two portions of the lava field.

Depth Range: 1300-1500 meters
