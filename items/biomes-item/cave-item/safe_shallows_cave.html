Safe Shallows Caves

The Safe Shallows Caves are a series of minor cave systems scattered around the Safe Shallows, the majority of which are too small for a Seamoth to navigate. While they can be a treasure trove of resources, they are also home to one of the two defensive fauna found in the Safe Shallows, the Crashfish, which can easily outnumber and kill a player without a Reinforced Dive Suit, Seaglide, or propulsion/repulsion cannon if they are not explored with caution. Limestone and sandstone outcrops can be seen in this area.

Depth Range: 10-75 meters
