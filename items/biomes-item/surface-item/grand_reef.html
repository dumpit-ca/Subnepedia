Grand Reef

The Grand Reef is home to a small amount of species, including two huge Ghost Leviathans and large bottom-dwelling Sea Treader Leviathans. It's also notable for its unusual flora, which includes the many bright Anchor Pods and the strange Membrain Trees. In the Grand Reef, there are several entrances to small caverns known as the Grand Reef Caves. Multiple hydrothermal vents can be found deep inside the Grand Reef. The Deep Grand Reef is a huge cavern system beneath this location with similar types of flora and wildlife, but with the addition of Crabsquids and Jellyrays and the exclusion of Sea Treader Leviathans. The Grand Reef is home to rubies, gel sacks, and a variety of other treasures.

Depth Range: 150-500 meters

Temperature Range: 13.5℃-16.1℃
