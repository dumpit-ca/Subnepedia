Bulb Zone

The Bulb Zone is distinguished by tiny fissures and overhangs in a sandy dune-like environment. It also has a lot of microscopic brown algae and a lot of plants with blueish purple tints, the most noteworthy of which being the Bulb Bush.

Certain harmful creatures can be found in the Bulb Zone. Notable predators like Bonesharks and Ampeels may be found all across the Bulb Zone. Mesmers are occasionally encountered.

Depth Range: 140 - 480 meters

Temperature: 13.8°C-14.1°C
