Mountains

The Mountains are distinguished by a variety of massive rocky mountain ranges, some of which have hydrothermal vents - magnetite is commonly found around these. The Mountains should be approached with extreme caution, as they are home to seven Reaper Leviathans. At least one can be located near the Mushroom Forest's island border. These behemoths may deter players from venturing deeper into the biome, where the best fragments and locations can be found. Here you'll find Wreck 5 and Wreck 19. The Mountain Island, located in the biome's southwest corner, rises 110 meters above sea level and contains numerous underwater Mountains Caves that can be explored either from the underwater entrance or from the island's coast.

Depth Range: 0-500 meters

Temperature Range: 13.6℃-14.8℃
