Mushroom Forest

The smallest of the two Mushroom Forests is the Northeastern Mushroom Forest. It can be found near the front of the Aurora, almost beneath it. It's densely forested with Tree Mushrooms and has a few cliffs leading down to the Bulb Zone. The majority of the Bulb Zone's Fauna, including its predators (Mesmers, Ampeels, and Bonesharks), will frequently travel into the Northeastern Mushroom Forest, making it just as deadly as the Northwestern Mushroom Forest. This environment is accessible to a Reaper Leviathan that has wandered out from the Crash Zone. In addition, there is usually a Reaper Leviathan on the Mountains' border, as well as a Kelp Forest near a naturally formed bridge.

Depth Range: 125-250 meters
