Sparse Reef

The Sparse Reef is a biome that borders one of the Grassy Plateaus, the Blood Kelp Trench, the Sea Treader's Path, the Grand Reef, and Kelp Forests southwest of Lifepod 5. As the name suggests, this area is sparse, with only a few species of minor animals and vegetation.The Deep Sparse Reef cave system is also located there. In this biome, it can be found near the wreck.

Depth Range: 40-310 meters

Temperature Range: 13.7℃-29.8℃
