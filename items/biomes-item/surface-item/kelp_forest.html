Kelp Forest

The Kelp Forest is one of Planet 4546B's most prolific and active ecosystems, as well as the major habitat for Stalkers and Hoverfish. With the exception of Stalkers and Drooping Stingers, it is located at medium-shallow depths and is home to largely passive living types. The seafloor is largely mossy and sandy, with a landscape of grassy knolls and arches that occasionally reveal access to small cavern systems. There are numerous canyons in these woodlands as well. On the sandy, grassy bottom, there is a lot of decorative flora.

Depth Range: 0-160 meters

Temperature Range: 24.3℃-27.5℃
