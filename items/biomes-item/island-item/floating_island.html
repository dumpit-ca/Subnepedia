Floating Island

In Subnautica, the Floating Island is a unique island habitat. It can be found southwest of Aurora. It is a massive landmass entirely supported by Ancient Floaters beneath the island. The climate is tropical, with abundant flora and a surface temperature that rarely falls below 41°C and never rises beyond 20°C.The Floating Island served as a meeting site for Aurora survivors, but only two reached it: Second Officer Keen and CTO Yu. Keen had left a signal for the Lifepod 19 rendezvous spot. An abandoned PDA is laying on the sand directly beneath the signal. For a short time, the Degasi Survivors also lived on the Floating Island. They built a primary base there as well.

Depth Range: Above sea level

Temperature Range: 20.2℃-40.9℃
