Mountain Island

Mountain Island is a vast biome on an island. It is situated on the highest point of a mountain range in the Mountains. It is near to the Bulb Zone, Kelp Forest, Mushroom Forest, and the Underwater Islands, and is located north of Lifepod 5. At its northern extremity, the Mountain Island also houses the Quarantine Enforcement Platform. The fog shrouds the island, so it isn't visible until you get close to it. The Sunbeam, on the other hand, can provide the player with geographic coordinates.

Depth Range: Above sea level

Temperature Range: 20.5℃-30.3℃
