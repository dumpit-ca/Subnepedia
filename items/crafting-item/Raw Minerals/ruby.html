Ruby

Ruby is a common raw material found on the Grand Reef, Lost River, and Deep Sparse Reef. It is used to manufacture and modify advanced vehicles. Thermal vents are the most likely place to find it.

Obtain From: Seabed

Location: Blood Kelp Zone, Dunes, Sea Treader's Path
