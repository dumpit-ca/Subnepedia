Gold

Gold is a raw material found in outcrops of shale and sandstone. It is a highly conductive metal that is utilized in the production of electronics and computers.Gold is also a significant resource deposit. The Jellyshroom Caves and Lost River are particularly rich in huge deposits. In the Inactive Lava Zone, single bits of raw gold can be found on walls and the ground.

Obtain From: Large Resource Deposits, Sandstone Outcrop, Shale Outcrop

Location: Grassy Plateaus, Mountains, Sea Treader's Path
