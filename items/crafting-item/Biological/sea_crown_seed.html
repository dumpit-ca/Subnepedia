Sea Crown

Only a few Sea Crowns occur in Subnautica, making it one of the rarest types of harvestable flora. After the Giant Cove Tree and the Fern Palm, it is the third rarest type of vegetation in Subnautica. The Bulb Zone, the Dunes Sinkhole, the entrance to the Jellyshroom caves, the Sea Emperor Leviathan's Aquarium, and specific Grassy Plateaus Caves are the only places where it may be found. In the general region of Lifepod 12, one of these plants can be discovered. Sea Crown Seeds can be harvested by cutting the plant with a knife.

Obtain From: Sea Crown

Location: Bulb Zone, Dunes, Grassy Plateaus Caves
