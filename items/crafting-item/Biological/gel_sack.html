Gel Sack

The Gel Sack is a type of edible flora that may be used to make Aerogel. It is typically found clinging to the cave walls. Using a Knife, it can be picked up or harvested for Gel Sack Spores. Per knife strike, a Gel Sack gives one Spore. This method can harvest up to three Spores from a single Gel Sack, with the third strike destroying the Sack. If a Gel Sack is picked up, extracting spores has no effect on its quality as a raw material or food source. There is a bug that can be exploited by hitting it twice with a knife, putting it in your inventory, and then hitting it again.

Obtain From: Seabed

Location: Mountains, Dunes, Lost River
