Bulb Bush Sample

Bulb Bush Samples are raw materials obtained from Bulb Bushes. It can be grown in the Alien Containment and the Exterior Growbed. A Pygmy Bulb Bush will result. It can be eaten raw, providing 3 Food and 10 Water to the player. When inserted in the Bioreactor, it generates 420 energy in total in 504 seconds.

Obtain From: Bulb Bush

Location: Bulb Zone
