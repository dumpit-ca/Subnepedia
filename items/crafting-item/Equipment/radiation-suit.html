Radiation Suit

The Radiation Suit is a piece of gear that protects players against radiation damage. After the Aurora has detonated, the blueprint is automatically accessible. The Radiation Suit, Radiation Gloves, and Radiation Helmet are the three parts that make up the Radiation Suit. When the player makes the Radiation Suit in the Fabricator, all three parts are added to the Inventory. To take effect, the Radiation Suit components must be placed in their relevant Paperdoll UI spaces. When not in use, all of the suit's parts occupy up four inventory slots (22).
