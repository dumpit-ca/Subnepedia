Purple Tablet

The Purple Tablet is the most common Alien Tablet encountered, and it can be found in a variety of locales.

In the game, there are presently seven working tablets.

To access the upper entrance or the anti-gravity elevator, as well as the control room, the Quarantine Enforcement Platform requires two people. Three Purple Tablets can be found on Mountain Island, two in the Mountain Island Caves, and one within the facility in the room before the deactivation interface. The seventh is at the Degasi Seabase on the Floating Island, which Marguerit Maida discovered and the other Degasi survivors explored.
