Orange Tablet

The rarest Alien Tablet is the Orange Tablet. It can be found on a desk in the Deep Grand Reef Degasi Seabase, and is thought to be what Marguerit Maida discovered, as depicted in the Degasi Log "Curious Discovery." This Alien Tablet can only be used to access the Lost River Laboratory Cache. After scanning it for the blueprint, the Orange Tablet can be created, although only one terminal requires the artifact.
