The Standard O2 Tank is a crafted item that increases the player's Oxygen supply by 30 units, giving them a total of 75 units. At the Fabricator, it is created.

To take advantage of the Standard O2 Tank, the player must place it in the Paperdoll UI's tank slot. It takes up six (23) slots in the inventory while not equipped. It may be modified at the Fabricator into the High Capacity O2 Tank, which can then be upgraded at the Modification Station into the Lightweight High Capacity Tank or the Ultra High Capacity Tank.
