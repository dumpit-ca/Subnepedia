Lightweight High Capacity Tank

The Lightweight High Capacity Tank is an accessory and upgrade to the High Capacity O2 Tank. After retrieving its blueprint from a Data Box, it can be constructed at the Modification Station. The prerequisite for a Plasteel Ingot can be difficult to meet early on in the game because Lithium is scarce. It enhances the player's oxygen supply by 90 units, for a total of 135 units, just like the High Capacity O2 Tank. However, because it is lighter than the High Capacity O2 Tank, it has no effect on swimming speed.
