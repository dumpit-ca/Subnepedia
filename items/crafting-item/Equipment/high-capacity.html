High Capacity O₂ Tank

The High Capacity O2 Tank is a Fabricator-crafted upgrade to the Standard O2 Tank, and it is the only upgrade that does not require a Modification Station. Only once the player has obtained a Standard O2 Tank can it be constructed. It increases the player's maximum Oxygen supply by 90 units, for a total of 135 units, while equipped.
