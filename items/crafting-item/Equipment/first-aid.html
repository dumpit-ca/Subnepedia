Cold Suit

The First Aid Kit is a piece of medical equipment that is used to help people recover their health. It's made with the Fabricator. There is one First Aid Kit in Lifepod 5's storage, with others in Supply Crates and on the Aurora.

It can also be gotten from a Medical Kit Fabricator, which produces one First Aid Kit every 10 minutes regardless of whether or not it is powered. The player can find one working Medical Kit Fabricator within Lifepod 5, and others can be built in a Seabase module or a Cyclops.
