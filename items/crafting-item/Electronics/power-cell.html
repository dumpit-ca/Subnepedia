Power Cell

The Power Cell is a type of electronics that may be created in the Fabricator. Power Cells may be produced only after the player has made a Battery. The Power Cell mostly powers automobiles.

A Power Cell can store 200 watts of electricity. Vehicles require Power Cells to operate and will deplete their energy as they are used. If they are exhausted or removed, the vehicle will stop working and will also stop producing oxygen. When running any power-draining equipment constructed within the Cyclops, as well as charging a vehicle in its docking bay, the Cyclops will drain energy.
