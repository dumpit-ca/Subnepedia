Ion Battery

TThe Ion Battery is an electrical component that may be made using the Fabricator. It works exactly like a regular battery, but it contains five times as much energy and takes five times as long to completely charge owing to its greater capacity.

It is unlocked by gathering data from the Alien Thermal Plant's Orange Data Terminal.
