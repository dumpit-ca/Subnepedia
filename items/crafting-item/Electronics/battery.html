Battery

The Advanced Wiring Kit is a constructed item in the Fabricator's electronics tree. Advanced Wiring Kits may be made only once the player has made a Computer Chip.

Batteries are utilized to create electronic instruments and equipment by giving energy. Batteries have a capacity of 100 mAh, which is depleted when utilizing battery-powered equipment.

Location: Crash Zone, Wrecks, Bulb Zone

=======

Bulb Zone

Small fissures and overhangs in a sandy dune-like environment characterize the Bulb Zone. Small brown algae abound, as well as a wide range of plants with blueish purple tints, the most noteworthy of which is the Bulb Bush. Certain harmful creatures can be found in the Bulb Zone. The Bulb Zone is home to a number of notable predators, including Bonesharks and Ampeels. Mesmers can also be found on occasion. Radiation affects the southern side of the Bulb Zone due to its proximity to the Crash Zone. A Radiation Suit is required to explore the southern region if the Aurora's drive room has not been rebuilt. Below the Bulb Zone lies a cave system known as the Bulb Zone.

Depth Range: 140-480 meters

Temperature Range: 13.8℃-14.1℃
