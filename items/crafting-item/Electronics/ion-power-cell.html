Ion Power Cell

Ion Power Cells are electrical objects that may be made using the Fabricator. They operate in the same way as standard Power Cells, but have five times the capacity and take five times longer to charge.

A single Ion Power Cell can store up to 1000 watts of energy.

It is unlocked by gathering data from the Alien Thermal Plant's Orange Data Terminal.
