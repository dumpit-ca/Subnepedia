Thumper

The Thumper is a deployable gadget that emits loud, pulsing noises to keep Ice Worms at bay. The Thumper prevents Ice Worms from emerging from the ground inside its radius, allowing the player to move through Ice Worm territory as noisily as they like without being noticed. The Thumpers have a small effect area and use a lot of battery power. Thumpers may allow the use of drills in the future without the noise attracting worms.
