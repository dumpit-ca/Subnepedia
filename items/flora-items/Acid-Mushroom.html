Acid Mushroom

Acid Mushrooms are a dangerous flora species. They are used to make batteries as a raw material. They can be picked by hand by the player. A Survival Knife can also be used to kill one and harvest four Acid Mushroom Spores, which discharge acid into the water, causing damage to the player, structures, and adjacent wildlife and vegetation.
