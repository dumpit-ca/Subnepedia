Purple Pineone

he Purple Pinecone is a relatively rare plant that serves as a harvesting node. Without the need of any instruments, it can be harvested for salt deposits. In the Northwestern Mushroom Forest, two can always be found southeast of Wreck 9. Purple Pinecones cannot be scanned, hence there is no databank entry for them.

Use: Crating Batteri

    Biome:

  • Northwestern Mushroom Forest
