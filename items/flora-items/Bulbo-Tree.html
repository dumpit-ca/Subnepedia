Bulb Tree

Bulbo Trees are a type of terrestrial vegetation that may be found on both the Floating Island and the Mountain Island. With a Survival Knife, the player can harvest Bulbo Tree Samples. Bulbo Tree Samples are edible and a viable food and water source. Until the 9th slash, which produces two samples and destroys the tree, each individual slash with the Survival Knife yields one sample.
