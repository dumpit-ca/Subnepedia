Bulb Bush

Bulb Bushes are a fast-growing vegetation species that can only be found in the Bulb Zone. Pygmy, Common, Large, and Giant are the four sizes available. Pygmy Bulb Bushes can be found both in the Bulb Zone and by planting a Bulb Bush in an Exterior Growbed or Alien Containment, but the Common, Large, and Giant Bulb Bushes can only be found in the Bulb Zone. Bulb Bushes can be harvested with a knife for their delicious samples. It can also be utilized in a variety of other crafting recipes.

Use: Edible, Crafting Hatching Enzymes
