Giant Cove Tree

The Flora of the Giant Cove Tree is one of a kind. The lone specimen known to exist can be discovered at the Lost River's Tree Cove. The saline around the tree is all blue, rather than the green brine seen elsewhere in the Lost River; it's unclear whether the Giant Cove Tree is to blame for the change in hue.
