Pink Cap

The Pink Cap is a terrestrial flora species that can be found in large numbers on both the Floating Island and the Mountain Island. The spores of this inedible mushroom can be picked with the survival knife or the thermoblade. Interior Growbeds, Exterior Growbeds, Plant Pots, and Plant Shelves are all good places to start.

Use: Decorative

    Biome:

  • Crag Field Cache
  • Floating Island
  • Mountain Island
