Oculus

The Oculus is a tiny, passive fauna species that can only be found in the Jellyshroom Cave. It can be caught and eaten fresh, roasted, or cured. The Oculus has the best energy to size ratio of any item in the game, with 630 energy in the Bioreactor and one inventory space.

Health: 20

Active During: Cathemeral
