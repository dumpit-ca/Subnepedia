Eyeye

The Eyeye is a herbivorous fauna species that is small and passive. It is edible and can be caught. It has mild food attributes and does not dehydrate when consumed, making it a suitable food source for players who are unable to cook. Eyeyes are widespread and can be found in a variety of biomes, as well as spawning in enormous numbers in the Jellyshroom Cave. It's worth noting that when eating an eyeye uncooked, no water is lost.

Health: 30

Active During: Diurnal
