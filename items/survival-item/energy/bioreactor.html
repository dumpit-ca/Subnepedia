Bioreactor

The Bioreactor is made with Subnautica's Habitat Builder and composts organic matter into energy. It contains a 44 inventory grid in which biological things can be stored, and once placed, items cannot be withdrawn. It can convert any organic substance into consistent energy for Seabases (Subnautica). With a maximum capacity of 500 units per Bioreactor, the Bioreactor produces 1 energy every 1.2 seconds (50 per minute). It can only be used in the Multipurpose Room's center (Subnautica). In creative mode, it has no effect on power.

Capacity : 500

Power/min: 50
