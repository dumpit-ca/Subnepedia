Thermal Plant

The Thermal Plant is a Habitat Builder-created generator that turns high temperatures (>25°C) into energy. To get the ingredient list for this object, you'll need to scan two sections. Given enough time and proximity to heat, the Thermal Plant can provide endless power to Seabases. A screen on the Thermal Plant shows the temperature of the surrounding area (rounded to whole degrees). The energy production per minute is linear and can be estimated using °C*1.32-33: so the energy output is zero at 25°C, 33 at 50°C, 66 at 75°C, 77.88 at 84°C, and 104 at 104°C. At any given time, a Thermal Plant can store up to 250 kWh of energy.

Capacity : 250

Power/min: 66 at 75°C
