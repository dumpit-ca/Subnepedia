Disinfected Water

Disinfected Water, like its less-filling equivalents Coffee Americano, Reclaimed Water, and Filtered Water, as well as its more-filling counterpart, the Large Filtered Water, replenishes the player's H2O levels. It holds 12 oz of water, which is roughly 340 grams.ug, according to the in-game description.
