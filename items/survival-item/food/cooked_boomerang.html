Cooked Boomerang

The Boomerang is a little fish with a bright yellow-green core and dark green stripes running down its sides and borders. It has horizontal symmetry and resembles a true boomerang in shape, as its name suggests. Long, slender, pointed fins protrude from the Boomerang's top and bottom, pointing diagonally rearward from its main body. These fins have a blue bioluminescent glow at their tips. It has four eyes, two on each side of its skull, above and below its mouth, which, despite being a herbivore, is full of sharp teeth for eating coral, according to the PDA.
