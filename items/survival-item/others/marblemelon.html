Marblemelon

The Marblemelon Plant provides the Marblemelon. Outside of a Degasi Seabase, it can be found in planters. The Marblemelon can be grown on a farm. The Small Marblemelon is a smaller version of the Marblemelon that only takes up one inventory slot. Marblemelons can be grown as complete plants in an Interior Growbed or hit with a Survival Knife to yield four Marblemelon Seeds. The plant is destroyed in a single strike, and all seeds are put to the player's inventory at once; any surplus seeds are lost if the player's inventory can't accommodate them. After being sown, seeds will take time to mature into adult plants.
