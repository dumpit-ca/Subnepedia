Snacks

Food products obtained from the vending machine are known as snacks. They have a poor Food value and deplete the player's Water, but they are infinite and free, which makes them beneficial in some scenarios. Despite the fact that they reduce Water values, they do not revert to their previous state or decompose, allowing them to be stored eternally. Snacks come in three different flavors, each with its own packaging but the same nutritional value. The Alterra logo appears on rice snacks. On the top right of Mix chips and on the left of Potato mix are the logos of an unknown manufacturer.
