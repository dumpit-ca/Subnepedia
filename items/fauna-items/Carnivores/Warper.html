Warper

The Warper, also known as the Self-Warping Quarantine Enforcer Unit by the Precursors, is a bio-mechanical living form that belongs to the Fauna category and was created by the Precursor race. An inactive Warper, as well as numerous Warper pieces, can be discovered in the Disease Research Facility in the Lost River, where they were being built before being halted in the middle of their construction due to the facility's attack by the Sea Dragon Leviathan.
