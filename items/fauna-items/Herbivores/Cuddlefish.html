Cuddlefish

The Cuddlefish is a friendly species of fauna that can be obtained only by hatching one of five Cuddlefish Eggs located throughout the game. It takes roughly 20 minutes for a real egg to hatch.

Attitude: Passive

Biome: Eggs, Deep Grand Reef Degasi Base, Dunes Sinkhole, Lost River Laboratory Cache, Mushroom Forest Caves
