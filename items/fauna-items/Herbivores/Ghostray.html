Ghostray

The Ghostray is a type of passive fauna that spawns in a variety of locations surrounding the Lost River. The Ghostray makes a droning, loud noise.

Attitude: Passive

    Biome:

  • Bone Fields
  • Ghost Canyon
  • Ghost Forest
