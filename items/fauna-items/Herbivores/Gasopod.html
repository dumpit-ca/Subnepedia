Gasopod

The Gasopod is a huge, herbivorous, defensive fauna species. It may be found leisurely traveling through the ocean in the Safe Shallows, Crash Zone Mesas, and Dunes vents. By discharging yellow, acidic Gas Pods from its tail, the Gasopod can inflict damage on the player. If the player is quick enough, they can be collected before they explode and turn into a lethal underwater mist.

Attitude: Passive

    Biome:

  • Crash Zone Mesas
  • Dunes
  • Safe Shallows
