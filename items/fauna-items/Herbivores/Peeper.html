Peeper

The Peeper is a small but quick species of passive fauna. It might be called Subnautica's mascot. It's easy to spot thanks to its huge, bright yellow eyes. Most life things larger than itself, such as Stalkers or the player, will strive to flee the Peeper. They can be captured, but their speed allows them to easily flee a player with insufficient equipment. They can be readily captured using a Grav Trap, Seaglide, Stasis Rifle, or Propulsion Cannon.

Attitude: Passive
