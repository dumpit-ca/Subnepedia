Bladderfish

The Bladderfish is a small, inactive species of fauna. It may be caught and eaten, and it can be found largely in the Safe Shallows and the Grand Reef. In compared to other little fish, the Bladderfish stands out because it can be utilized to make Filtered Water. Bladderfish, as a result, are a decent source of water, though not as good as Disinfected Water or Large Filtered Water.

Attitude: Passive

Biome: Blood Kelp Caves, Crash Zone Mesas, Dunes, Grand Reef, Grassy Plateaus, Kelp Forest, Lost River, Tree Cove Mushroom Forest, Primary Containment Facility Aquarium, Safe Shallows
