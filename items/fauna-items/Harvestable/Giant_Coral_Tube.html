Giant Coral Tubes

Giant Coral Tubes are a type of coral that may be found in the biomes of Safe Shallows, Crash Zone, and Kelp Forest. When hit with a knife, it gives the player Coral Tube Samples. They have a health of 50000. The Giant Coral Tube will be destroyed if hit enough times. However, unless done deliberately, it is extremely unlikely to ever destroy one. It takes 2500 knife strikes to reach 50000 HP (1250 with a Thermoblade). When the player gets far enough away from the Coral Tube, the lost HP is forgotten and the player's HP is restored to the full 50000.

Attitude: Passive

    Biome:

  • Crash Zone
  • Kelp Forest
  • Primary Containment Facility Aquarium
  • Safe Shallows
  • Safe Shallows Caves
