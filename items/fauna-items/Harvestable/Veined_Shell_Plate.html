Veined Shell Plate

A common coral species is the Coral Shell Plate. There are three types: coral, slanted, and veined. When hit a few times, the initial one is the only one that can be harvested, and it can yield anywhere from 1 to 4 Coral Tube Samples. Despite the fact that the latter two are of no practical use and drop nothing, they all share a data bank record.

Attitude: Passive
