Pyrocoral

Pyrocoral is a mossy material wrapped around a grey rock structure with a large base that branches into many pieces. The coral is green in hue and is coiled around the rock like a net. It has a slimy appearance. Exposed lava can be visible at the tips of the rock's branching pieces, giving off a faint yellow glow. Only the Propulsion/Repulsion Cannon may interface with it.

Attitude: Passive
