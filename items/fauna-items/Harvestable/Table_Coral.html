Table Coral

Table corals are available in four colors: red, green, blue, and purple. The red version is significantly more frequent than the others, and may be found in the Safe Shallows with ease. They have a disk form with multiple little glowing "jewels" on top and bottom. Table corals are commonly found adhering to walls, however the majority of species can be seen rising upwards from the seabed. Pulsation has been observed in table corals.

Attitude: Passive
