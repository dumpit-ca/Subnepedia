Ampeel

The amoeboid is a quiet, stationary fauna species that is found in abundance in practically all sections of the Lost River, frequently in groups. Amoeboids can be scanned or seized with a propulsion gun, but they can't be interacted with.

Attitude: Passive

Biome: Disease Research Facility, Lost River, Bone Fields, Ghost Canyon, Ghost Forest, Junction, Mountains Corridor, Lost River Laboratory Cache
