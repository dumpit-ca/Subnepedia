Floater

The Ancient Floaters' juvenile form is known as Floaters. Floaters are a type of fauna that lives in the Safe Shallows and Grassy Plateaus. They can be captured and attached to objects and props, although not all of them will float after being attached. They will try to attach themselves to the player at times. The player's health will be reduced by 10%, and the Floater will die. Floaters can be held in the hands of the players and attached to various items. Floaters can be used to shift rocks that obstruct cave entrances on occasion.

Attitude: Aggressive

Health: 40

    Damage:

  • Bite: 5

    Biomes:

  • Grassy Plateaus
  • Safe Shallows
