Lava Larva

The Lava Larva is a parasitic fauna species of medium size. The body of the Lava Larva is dark violet and somewhat flattened on top and underside. On the back, there is a striped pattern. While emitting high-pitched chirping sounds, the Lava Larva will swim around aimlessly and retreat from any predators. It will cling to the Seamoth, Prawn Suit, and Cyclops, sucking their energy and slowing down the vehicle.

Attitude: Passive

Health: 100
