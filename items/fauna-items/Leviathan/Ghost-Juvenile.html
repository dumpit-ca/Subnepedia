Ghost Leviathan Juveniles

The young of Ghost Leviathans are known as Ghost Leviathan Juveniles. They are roughly 63 percent the size of a mature human living within The Crater, but they have the same level of health as their adult counterparts. Juveniles, on the other hand, do 34.5 percent less damage.
