Sea Dragon

The Sea Dragon Leviathan belongs to the giant leviathan class of fauna. It is Subnautica's largest and most ferocious beast. There are three Sea Dragon Leviathans on the map, one in the Lava Lakes and two in the Inactive Lava Zone.

Attitude: Aggressive

Health: 5000

    Biomes:

  • Blood Kelp Zone
  • Inactive Lava Zone
  • Lava Lakes

    Damage:

  • Bite: 10-20 (Prawn Suit)

    • Swat:

  • Swat: 40 (Player)
  • Swat: 11 (Prawn Suit)
  • Swat: 250 (Cyclops)

    • Fire Breath:

  • Player: 12-72
  • Prawn Suit: 3-19
  • Cyclops: 500
