Welcome to Subnepedia

Unknown Worlds Entertainment developed and published Subnautica, an open-world survival action-adventure computer game. After their spaceship, the Aurora, crashes on the alien planet 4546B's surface, players are free to explore the ocean. Open world full of wonder and peril waits you!
