About Subnepdia

Get To Know Us

Subnepedia is an infographic website based on the game Subnautica. It was created to give players information about the mechanics of the game, and the elements of the game that most players will need to know. Subnepedia also serves to let the players know what to expect with the game, and offers useful tips for the players to use throughout the game.
