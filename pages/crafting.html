Crafting

Overview

In Subnautica, you can make a lot of different things. Everything from basic materials to food to electronics to vehicles can be included. It can be tough to remember or even find recipes when there is such a wide range of possibilities for crafting and creating new things.


Crafting Guide will ensure that you are familiar with practically all of the game's recipes. This will be your go-to resource for learning how to make the next thing you'll need for survival.


Basic Materials, Sustenance, Vehicles, and Scanner Room are among the categories in which the things are classified. You need each of these categories and you must know how to put together anything you need from a specific one. Because you'll be using the Fabricator to make the majority of your items, it's better if you know how to make one first. To make a Fabricator, mix Titanium, Gold, and Table Coral Sample to make a Builder, which can then be transformed into a Fabricator. After that, you can concentrate on crafting food, tools, equipment, and machines using it.

Materials

Subnautica contains a wide variety of materials, ranging from basic titanium to rare and valuable diamonds. However, with the exception of mercury, most of the resources can be used in crafting recipes. Outcrops provide the next set of very useful materials (limestone, sandstone, basalt, shale, and so on). These materials are helpful in the ways that they are used. They can be used to make electronic components such as copper wire and computer chips, which are extremely useful. Quartz is also useful since it can be used to manufacture glass, which can be utilized to make survival tools and base building components.Lithium, diamonds, and a variety of other commodities can be found in Subnautica. Electronics

Electronics are created by combining two or more elements using the Fabricator.

Tools

Tools are devices that can be made from Basic or Advanced Materials with the Fabricator or enhanced from other Tools with the Modification Station. They may be found in the Inventory and equipped in the Toolbar. The majority of them are powered by batteries. The Flashlight, Habitat Builder, Laser Cutter, Pathfinder Tool, Propulsion Cannon, Repair Tool, Repulsion Cannon, Scanner, and Stasis Rifle are among these tools. Some Tools can only be generated via Console Commands right now.
