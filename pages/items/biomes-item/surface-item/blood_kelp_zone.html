Blood Kelp Zone Trench

Between the Dunes, the Sparse Reef, the Grassy Plateaus, and the Sea Treader's Path, the Blood Kelp Trench is located south-west of Lifepod 5. The Trench lacks the Northern Blood Kelp Zone's Crabsquids and Ghost Leviathan, but it does have predators in the shape of Blighters, Ampeels, and Warpers. If one is not careful, the more limited places may make it more difficult to avoid them.It consists of a single deep trench with natural rock bridges crossing from one wall to the next, as well as a minor cavern system beneath the Bloodvines' roots. In this area, one of the Wrecks is wedged between the trench's walls. This zone is not recommended for Cyclops exploration.

Depth Range: 150-160 meters

Temperature Range: 13.5℃-14.3℃
