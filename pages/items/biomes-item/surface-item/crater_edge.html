Crater Edge

Subnautica takes place inside The Crater, a massive, dormant aquatic volcano with a circumference of almost two kilometers. The area surrounding this crater is known as the Crater Edge, or the Void. The zone serves as a barrier for the game's map, as it has no resources and is extremely dangerous.Adult Ghost Leviathans can be found in the Crater Edge, where they prevent players from leaving the playable region. When you initially enter the Crater Edge, a single Ghost Leviathan appears. A second Ghost Leviathan will spawn if the player stays in the Crater Edge for 30 seconds. If you stay in the area for another 30 seconds, a third and final Ghost Leviathan will appear. They will pursue the player until they return to the playable area, at which point they will be killed.

Depth Range: 0-8192 meters

Temperature Range: 10.2℃-11.5℃
