Blood Kelp Caves

Big Bloodroots grow on the walls and ceilings of these caverns, carrying large pustules of Blood Oil on their strong stems. There is also a lot of bioluminescent vegetation and cavern structures in the caves (stalagmites and stalactites). Small Bloodvine stems abound in the area, and their bioluminescent roots are always visible.

Depth Range: 285-675 meters
