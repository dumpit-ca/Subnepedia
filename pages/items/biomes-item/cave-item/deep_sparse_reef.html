Deep Sparse Reef

The Deep Sparse Reef is home to vast groups of Shuttlebugs, Eye Stalks, and Tiger Plants, as well as several deep pits and interweaving cave systems. An entry to the Blood Kelp Trench can be found deep within the area. The biome is mostly devoid of life.Southwest of Lifepod 5 is the Deep Sparse Reef. Second Officer Keen took refuge in the trench, where Lifepod 19 was also discovered.

Depth Range: 210-290 meters
