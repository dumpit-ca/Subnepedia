Chinese Potato

The Chinese Potato is an item that may be obtained by picking up Chinese Potato Plants (Subnautica). Exterior Growbeds, Interior Growbeds, and Planter Pots are all good options. It produces 140 energy in the Bioreactor (Subnautica) over the duration of 168 seconds. The player can ingest Chinese Potatoes, which provide 12 Food and 3 Water. Despite having fewer food points than Marblemelons, the Chinese Potato grows faster and produces a larger crop from a single seed.
