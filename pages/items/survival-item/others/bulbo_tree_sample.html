Bulbo Tree Sample

Bulbo Tree Samples are obtained by cutting down Bulbo Tree plants using a survival knife. It can be planted in the Planter Pots, the Interior Growbed, and the Exterior Growbed. It produces 420 energy in the Bioreactor during the duration of 504 seconds. It can be consumed, providing 8 points of food and 10 points of water to the player. Read the main Bulbo Tree article for a full estimate of the food/water value of Bulbo Trees against Marblemelons.
