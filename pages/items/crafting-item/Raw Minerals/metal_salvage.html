Metal Salvage

Metal Salvage is a raw resource that can be found strewn around the bottom, generally near the Aurora. Salvage of major portions of Alterra Corporation ships is prohibited by the PDA, however these salvages are permitted. Stalkers pick it up and gather it in the Kelp Forest. It should also be noted that if a Stalker picks up a Metal Salvage, the Stalker has a 25% chance of dropping a Stalker Tooth. However, one of the metal textures, which can be seen in figure #4 of the gallery, has a 0% chance of dropping a Stalker Tooth. This appears to be a bug.

Obtain From: Seabed

Location: Crag Field, Crash Zone, Dunes
