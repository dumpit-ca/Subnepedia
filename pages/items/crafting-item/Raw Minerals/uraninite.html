Uraninite Crystal

Uraninite Crystals are a raw material used to make the Reactor Rod, a single component (Subnautica). They can be found on the seafloor in raw form as well as in huge resource deposits.

Obtain From: Seabed, Large Resource Deposits

Location: Lost River, Grand Reef Caves, Underwater Islands
