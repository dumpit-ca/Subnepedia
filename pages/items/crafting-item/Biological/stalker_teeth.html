Stalker Teeth

When Stalkers pick up an object, generally Titanium or Metal Salvage, they may drop Stalker Teeth as a Raw Material. Dropping numerous Metal Salvage near the monster is a good way to get Stalker Teeth, as they sometimes leave a single tooth behind after picking up the Salvage. The Stalker will swim back and forth between two or three heaps that are close together practically endlessly. Placing Camera Drones near a Stalker, allowing a Stalker to assault a Seamoth or a Prawn Suit, crashing into Stalkers with a Seamoth or punching them with a Prawn Suit, or shooting things at them with a Propulsion Cannon are all options for accumulating teeth. A Grav Trap can also be used to keep the Stalkers close.

Obtain From: Seabed

Location: Kelp Forest
