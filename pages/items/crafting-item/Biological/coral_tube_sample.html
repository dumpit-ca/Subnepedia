Coral Tube Sample

The Coral Tube Sample is a raw material obtained by using the Survival Knife to harvest a Giant Coral Tube or a Coral Shell Plate. The Coral Tube Sample can be scanned, however the only way to do so is to collect one and drop it when harvested from a Giant Coral Tube or to scan a piece of Coral Shell Plate that comes off.

Obtain From: Giant Coral tubes, Coral Shell Plate

Location: Bulb Zone, Crash Zone, Kelp Forest
