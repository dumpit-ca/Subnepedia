Deep Shroom

The Deep Shroom is a dangerous flora species that may be found in small clusters in the Blood Kelp Zones and tunnels, as well as in the Lost River and Sea Treader's Tunnel Caves. It's a relative of the Acid Mushroom, and it's utilized to make Hydrochloric Acid as a raw material.

Obtain From: Seabed

Location: Blood Kelp Zone, Inactive Lava Zone, Sea Treader's Path
