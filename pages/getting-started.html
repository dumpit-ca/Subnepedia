Venturing The Ocean

When you are going out for an expedition, always bring only food, water, batteries, and tool you need. Taking few items will help you to have larger space in your inventory while in expedition.

Build, Build, Build

Five best location to build your bases are, between Grassy Plateaus and Safe Shallows, in Floating Island, underwater olives, Giant mushroom tree, and in the sea traders path. In these five locations you will have access in things you need to surive.

Tiny Spaces

Plants pots are better than Growbeds, plant pots consume smaller place than growbeds. In this game space is one of the important thing to consider.

Pin It!

Always carry atleast two beacons, the game does bot have in-game map, also the underwater biomes do not have eaxactly landmarks or signposts.

Don't Settle For Less

Focus on upgrading your oxygen tanks and get a rebreather if can because this thing allows you to dive deeper without your oxygen draining quicker

Basic Necessities

Never forget the one basic thing about this game. Nothing stacks in this game so everything you pick up takes up its own separate inventory slot.

Organize Freak

When you start building your base, build a bunch of lockers so you have plenty of storage for your materials.
