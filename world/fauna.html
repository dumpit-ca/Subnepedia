Fauna

Overview

Fauna refers to animals, specifically the species of World 4546B. Fauna may be found all around the planet, from the deep depths of the Crystal Caves to the lively Safe Shallows and the burning pits of the Lava Lakes.


On Planet 4546B, fauna come in various shapes, sizes, and forms. From the cave-dwelling Rockgrub to the massive Sea Dragon Leviathan, which spews molten materials from its mouth. Although formerly unable to thrive owing to the presence of the Kharaa Bacterium, the fauna of Planet 4546B is capable of enduring and adapting to many circumstances. Certain types of tiny animals can be spotted creating mainly non-interactive shoals on occasion. Yellow blood is also found in the fauna on 4546B. The fact that certain creatures on the planet seem similar to those on Earth may be explained as a simple instance of convergent evolution.
