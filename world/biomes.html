Biomes

Overview

Subnautica has a diverse set of Biomes to explore and exploit. They are all tiny replicas of real-world ecosystems. The Biomes range from the bright Kelp Forest to the boiling depths of the intriguing Lava Lakes. Each Biome has its own Flora and Fauna to discover as well as resources to harvest.
