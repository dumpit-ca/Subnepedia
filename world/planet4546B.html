Planet 4546B

Planet 4546B is found in the Ariadne Arm's furthest regions, circling the star 4546. The letter "B" indicates that 4546B was the first planet identified orbiting the star 4546. (or 4546A). The system contains at least eleven more celestial bodies, each of which is larger than Earth's Moon. Planet 4546B is a water planet with a livable oxygen-nitrogen atmosphere that is smaller than Earth. It also has two moons, one of which is relatively near to the planet and causes regular solar eclipses.


The planet is known to have three primary ecosystems: the Crater, Sector Zero, and the Dead Zone, the latter of which spans the bulk of the planet. Except for a few islands, the planet is almost entirely covered in water and has a variety of submerged biomes. The Aurora is placed in shallow water, a few meters above the surface.


The Crater has a rich plant and animal ecosystem. This is, however, a very small piece of the planet, located in a dormant volcanic crater around 2 kilometers in diameter. The remaining water is known as the Crater Edge, and it is extremely hazardous.


NOTE: On the right, you will see the crater's map which can guide you to navigate the planet
