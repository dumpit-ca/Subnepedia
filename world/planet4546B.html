Planet 4546B

Planet 4546B is a category-3 ocean planet that serves as the setting for Subnautica. The Planet was established by the Architects as a research station to investigate a bacteria known as the Kharaa Bacterium. The Kharaa Bacteria is a highly contagious extraterrestrial bacterium found by the Precursors while exploring another world and setting up research centers on planets to find a cure. One of these outposts is 4546B.

Overview

Planet 4546B is located in the Ariadne Arm's furthest regions, circling the star 4546. The letter "B" indicates that 4546B was the first planet identified orbiting the star 4546. (or 4546A). There are at least 10 more celestial bodies in the system, each larger than Earth's Moon.Planet 4546B is a water planet with a livable oxygen-nitrogen atmosphere. It also has two moons, one of which is relatively near to the planet and causes regular solar eclipses.


The planet is known to have three primary ecosystems: the Crater, Sector Zero, and the Dead Zone, the latter of which spans the majority of the planet. Except for a few islands, the known planet is almost entirely covered in water and has a variety of submerged biomes. The Aurora is placed in shallow water, a few meters above the surface.


The Crater is home to a diverse ecology of flora and fauna. However, this is a very tiny portion of the planet, lying in a dormant volcano crater around 2 kilometers in diameter. The remainder of the water is known as the Crater Edge, and it is highly dangerous.
