Flora

Overview

The plant and plant-like life of Planet 4546B ranges in size and appearance, from the brilliant Acid Mushrooms to the enigmatic Giant Cove Tree. Except for the Lava Lakes (because to their severe temperatures) and the Crater Edge. Flora may be found everywhere around the globe. The dead plants discovered at the bottom of the dead zone are the lone exception. They are stationary living forms that stay in one place unless altered with the Repulsion Cannon, Propulsion Cannon, knife, or other means.
